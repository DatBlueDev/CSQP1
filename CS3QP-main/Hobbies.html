Hobbies and interests

Programming is something I am really passionate about and enjoy. Creating things like Discord Bots, automations, macros, and even video games with Unity; I have come to enjoy the feeling of creating something with code, solving problems and providing solutions with my programs. I have also come to experience of banging my head against the wall (metaphorically) when my code doesn't work, and that amazing feeling like you just won the lottery when it does.

Sometimes after lazing around, it's a good idea to step out of one's cave and see the world. I do this by Cycling, I use my bike and go outside, maybe touch the grass and feel the breeze of fresh air. It's a great way to get some exercise, increase endurance, and a break from always being indoors.
