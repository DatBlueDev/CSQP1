Favorite Artists:

TUYU is a music group from Japan that sings about a variety of things, most notably personal problems people face, such as feelings of inferiority, being passionless, etc. Their most famous song, "Compared Child" is a song about being constantly being compared to others, and judged; feeling inferior and hopeless, that no matter how much they try it seems they can't succeed. Thankfully, it does end on a lighter note.

Inabakumori is actually my favorite artist among these, he creates songs with something called VOCALOID, which is basically an artificial voice. Inabakumori uses the vocaloid Kaai Yuki (though sometimes switches to others), whose voice is synthesized from a real 4th grader at the time. One of Inabakumori's most famous songs is Lagtrain; the lyrics are vague, but the general theme is about social anxiety and being left behind. Side note: the song and instrumentals is fire.

PinocchioP is another musician that utilizes VOCALOID, he mostly Hatsune Miku, (one of the most popular vocaloid) among others. One of his more popular songs and a personal favorite is "Reincarnation Apple". it is about not being able to accept oneself, always wanting for something else, to be something else, wanting to be perfect. Hence the theme reincarnation, but no matter how much they reincarnate, there will always be flaws, and in the end it is best to accept oneself - as seen in the final scene where they throw away the apple that reincarnates them.
